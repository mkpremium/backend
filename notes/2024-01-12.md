## Migration triggering

1. Deploy ECS with the following environment:
```
EVENTS_QUEUE_URL=https://sqs.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/173249668334/events-migration
DATABASE=postgres
TRIGGER_BUILDINGS_MIGRATION=true
TRIGGER_OPERATORS_MIGRATION=true
```
2. Select the "Main prod VPC" and remove the RDS subnets from the selection
